Steps to make Salsa Chicken:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°
  2. Place the frozen chicken on a sheet pan in the oven for 15 minutes.
  3. Pull the chicken out and cover it with salsa. Place back in the oven for 30 minutes.
  4. Pull the chicken out and cover it with cheese. Place back in the oven for 5 minutes.
  5. Plate and serve.
