The ingredients needed to make Sheet pan barbecue chicken and sausage:
  1. Prepare 6 frozen mini corn on the cob
  2. Take 1 bell pepper
  3. Get 2 zucchinis
  4. Take 1 red onion
  5. Take 2 pounds smoked sausage
  6. Prepare 1 pound chicken tenders
  7. Prepare 3 tablespoons olive oil
  8. Prepare 3 tablespoons honey
  9. Take 1 tablespoon chili powder
  10. Get 1 teaspoon paprika
  11. Prepare 1/2 bbq sauce
  12. Prepare Dinner rolls

Steps to make Sheet pan barbecue chicken and sausage:
  1. Wrap corn individually in aluminum foil
  2. Cut all veggies
  3. Please everything but corn in a Ziploc bag and mix or you can use a bowl
  4. Cover baking pan with aluminum foil pour mixture onto pan put the corn on the side, Bake 425 for 28 to 35 minutes or until chicken is cooked brush with barbecue sauce before serving serve with dinner rolls
