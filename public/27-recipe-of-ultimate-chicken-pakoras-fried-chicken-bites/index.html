The ingredients needed to make Chicken pakoras (fried chicken bites):
  1. Take 200 gm Chicken (cube cut)
  2. Make ready 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup rice-flour
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp cumin powder
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp garam masala powder
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp chilli powder
  8. Prepare Crushed peppercorns
