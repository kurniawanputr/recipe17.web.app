The ingredients needed to make Pineapple curry chicken soup:
  1. Take 1 can coconut cream
  2. Take 2 cups water
  3. Take 4 cups unsweetened coconut milk
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup coconut oil
  5. Take 2 chicken breast two thighs
  6. Make ready 1 big piece of ginger Minced
  7. Prepare 4 garlic cloves minced
  8. Get 25 sweet peppers
  9. Prepare 2 whole white Spanish onions
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup cilantro Chopped up
  11. Get 1/4 cup. Thai Basil
  12. Take 2 tablespoons Thai basil flowers
  13. Take 1 can pineapple
