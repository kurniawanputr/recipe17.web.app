The ingredients needed to make Chicken Alfredo with Grilled Apples:
  1. Make ready 4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (we only had 2)
  2. Make ready 1-2 large Braeburn or Gala apple, cut into 1/2-inch wedges
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  4. Take Alfredo sauce, warmed (we used 2 jars of our favorite sauce)
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese (for the top garnish when making plates or bowls) or can use shredded Parmesan (we used parmesan for the kids)
  6. Take Can mushrooms
  7. Take Fresh spinach
  8. Get Penne or Fettuccine or spaghetti whatever you have
  9. Get Shredded Parmesan for the Alfredo sauce optional
